Frequently asked questions

  • Can I get any other type of milk and sugar? What about the syrup?
  • Absolutely! We have whole milk, low-fat, lactose-free, cashew, oat, rice, soy and almond milk. Just keep in mind that they cost a bit more - around $1-1.50. We also offer white/brown sugar or stevia, sucralose, xylitol or aspartame - there is no price difference here. As for the syrup, we usually offer chocolate, vanilla, hazelnut, caramel and peppermint. Be on the lookout for other special flavors that are limited edition!
  • Can I get my order to go?
    Yes, definitely. You are free to enjoy your drink wherever and whenever you want, free of charge. We do, however, charge an additional $0.50 per food container. 
  • Do you take reservations? 
    We do not usually take reservations, but during busy or special days (holidays, birthdays, parties) you are invited to reserve your table so you can be sure that we'll save you a seat! :)
  • Can I bring the cats my own food/treats?
    As much as it saddens us to say this, unfortunately, you cannot. Our cats have sensitive stomachs and have been on a consistent and well-researched diet. The cat food from our menu is the same as they eat at home, so feel free to order something for your favorite fluffy paws and enjoy the feral goblin noises they make. :) 
  • Can I take the cats outside? 
    Well, not yet. Although our terrace may seem inviting, our cats are strictly indoors - but we are working on leash training them and transforming the terrace into a cat patio as well. Please be patient, soon you will be able to take them outside for fly hunting :)
